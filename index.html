Tic Tac Toe++

The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. You are X, your friend is O. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner! When all 9 squares are full, the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
